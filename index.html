MBS抗冲改性剂

IMPACT MODIFIERS FOR ENGINEERING PLASTICS

MBS类增韧剂采用丁二烯橡胶核/高Tg壳层的核壳结构，适用于PC及其合金体系的抗冲提升与性能平衡。围绕通用增韧、低温韧性、阻燃协同、耐水解以及再生料稳定化等需求，可提供较完整的选型路线。

MBS impact modifiers use a butadiene-rubber core-shell structure for PC and PC alloys. They cover general toughening, low-temperature impact, FR synergy, hydrolysis resistance and recycled-material stabilization.

核心优势
  • 核壳结构兼顾冲击吸收与基体相容性，适合PC、PC/ABS及PC/PBT等体系
  • 自由流动粉末，便于稳定分散、配方导入与连续加工
  • 可覆盖低温抗冲、阻燃协同、耐水解及再生料应用等方向
  • Core-shell architecture balances impact absorption with matrix compatibility.
  • Free-flowing powder supports consistent dispersion, easy formulation integration and stable processing.
  • Applicable to low-temperature toughness, flame-retardant synergy, hydrolysis resistance and recycled-content formulations.
